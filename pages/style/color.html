Text Color

Text color refers to the color applied to the text content of an element, such as paragraphs, headings, links, and other textual content. You can change the color of text using the color property in CSS.

  • text & -color type - is the class name used for text-color

    • Use Cases of Text Colors

    • Use font colors to emphasize critical information or key points.
    • Use a contrasting color to draw attention to calls to action (CTAs).
    • Use font color to differentiate headings from body text and establish a hierarchy.
    • Use brand colors in the text to maintain consistent branding across the website or marketing materials.
    • Use font colors to distinguish different types of content, like quoted text, instructions, or side notes.

    Background Color

    Background color refers to the color that is applied to the background of an element, such as a page, a section, a div, or even text. It helps in visually distinguishing different sections or elements, enhancing the design, and improving readability.

  • bg & -color type - is the class name used for background-color

    • Use Cases of Background Colors

    • Use background colors to enhance the contrast between text and the background for better readability, especially on light or dark themes.
    • Use brand colors for backgrounds to maintain consistency with the overall brand identity
    • Use background colors to highlight buttons, forms, or areas with calls to action.
    • Use background colors to highlight key phrases, definitions, or important points in the content./li>
