Paragraph

Paragraph is a block of text that is wrapped within the p tag. It is one of the most fundamental elements, used to structure and organize content by grouping related sentences together. Paragraphs help break up large bodies of text into manageable sections, improving readability and the overall flow of the content on a webpage.

  • p - is an abbreivation for paragraph

    • Use Cases of Paragraph

    • Use paragraphs to describe an idea, concept, or object in detail.
    • Divide large chunks of text into smaller sections for better readability.
    • Organize essays into an introduction, body paragraphs, and conclusion.

    Example

    This is a paragraph

    Sizes Class name
    1
    Lagos City
    		 .p

    Line break

    Inside a paragraph, if the text reaches the edge of the container, the browser automatically wraps the text to the next line.

  • br - is an abbreivation for line-break

    • Use Cases of Line Breaks

    • Use line breaks to display addresses in a readable format.
    • Present information in list form without using ul or ol
    • Provide brief instructions with line breaks for readability.
