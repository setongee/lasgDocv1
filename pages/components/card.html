Cards

Cards are a UI design pattern used to group and display related content in a visually distinct and interactive way. They typically consist of a container with an image, text, and actions like buttons or links.

They provide key information about the content (e.g., product name, article title).

Cards are widely used for their flexibility and ease of use in organizing and presenting content.
