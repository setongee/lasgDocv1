Dialogue Box

A dialogue box is a small, interactive pop-up window that appears on top of the main content to communicate important information or request user input. It typically requires the user to take action, such as confirming, canceling, or providing additional details.

Dialogue boxes are commonly used to ensure users make deliberate decisions or to provide important information without navigating away from the current page.
