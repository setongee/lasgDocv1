Button

Buttons are interactive elements in a user interface that allow users to perform actions or trigger events. They typically contain text or icons and are clickable or tappable.

Buttons often have different states such as normal, hover, active, and disabled to reflect their usability or current status.

Buttons are styled with colors, borders, and shadows to make them visually distinct and indicate interactivity.
