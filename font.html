Fonts

The preferred font style used by Lagos State is Plus Jakarta Sans, a modern and clean sans-serif typeface. It is chosen for its readability, versatility, and contemporary design, which aligns with the state's official communication style. Plus Jakarta Sans is often utilized in various public-facing materials, digital interfaces, and signage to maintain a consistent visual identity across government platforms and initiatives.

HTML

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS. 

                        
                          <link rel="preconnect" href="https://fonts.googleapis.com">
                          <link rel="preconnect" href="https://fonts.gstatic.com" crossorigin>
                          <link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Plus+Jakarta+Sans:ital,wght@0,200..800;1,200..800&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
